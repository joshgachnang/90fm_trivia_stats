Welcome to Trivia Stats

Trivia Stats is a web app tracking 90FM's World's Largest Trivia Contest.

You can track your scores, get notified when new scores are posted, compare yourself to other teams (soon), and look up past years. If anything doesn't work, send me an email. I built this site in a few hours, so it might still have bugs.

The site is built with Python and Django. You can view the source and contribute on Github.
